Faqs

Top 10 Frequently asked questions

  • Are all your Phones New and Original?

    • All our Phones, each one of them, which can be found on MOBI.com.pk page are 100% New and Original.

  • How can I buy a Phone at MOBI.com.pk?

    • For more information please visit our Brands page.

  • Is it possible for me to cancel my order and what would be the procedure of doing it?

    • a-Before customer pays for its Phone order can be canceled in any moment.

      b-If the payment transaction is finished, customer can still cancel its order by simply sending us an E-mail with all details of purchased model, explanation why he wants to cancel the purchase and its contact information.

      c-Also, order can be canceled without any additional charges while order is still in a possession of MOBI.com.pk and until the order or request for its delivery is not handed over to a courier service (procedure in detail in 1d.)

      d-If the order or request for its delivery has already been sent to the courier service, customer can still cancel the order, but in this case customer will be charged for all delivery costs and will also be obligated to send the order and all its accessories that he received within the order undamaged back to MOBI.com.pk at its own expense. After MOBI.com.pk receives the order back and its undamaged condition is recorded, customer will get money refund in time period defined in Terms and Conditions (Terms and Conditions Return Policy)

  • Can I buy the Phone in installments?

    • MOBI.com.pk does not have option for paying in installments nor is giving any kind of loans to its clients.

  • Can I order and buy a Phone from your website from any country?

    • No, MOBI.com.pk can only accept and ship orders in Cities that are part of the Pakistan.

  • How do the warranties work?

    • a-Pakistan Spec Phones have Pakistan warranty and this warranty is valid in every city of the Pakistan in official cell phone services.

      b-If customer is not able to repair its Phone in his own City for any reasons, he can ask for MOBI.com.pk to help him find one. MOBI.com.pk has agreements with some of the major official repair services in Pakistan and every phone that is purchased with the Pakistan warranty can be sent to one of these services to be repaired. All transportation costs are client's responsibility.

      c-If client purchased phone without Pakistan warranty, but with seller warranty, then he needs to contact our service team (warranty@MOBI.com.pk) and after that send the phone to MOBI.com.pk closest repair service.

  • Can you deliver in small towns and villages?

    • MOBI.com.pk can deliver on every address in the Pakistan that is covered by GLS.

  • Can I personally come and take over my order?

    • Yes, If you can live in Karachi so you can come MOBI.com.pk shop and pick up the order he placed on MOBI.com.pk shop.

  • Can I return my Phone, what is the procedure and what is the time period that I can expect my money to be refunded?

    • a-In cases that buyer within 14 days after receiving his order decides that he is not satisfied with the phone and wants to return it, he can do that by shipping the entire order, phone and all its accessories (please read 2b. for more details) back to MOBI.com.pk at its own expense and when MOBI.com.pk establishes that phone is intact and that the phone and all its accessories are properly returned, customer will get his money refunded.

      b-If you decide to return items from your order for any reason, before sending it back, please send an e-mail to MOBI.com.pk sales department with all information (phone details) as well as an explanation why would you like to return your order, together with your contact information at warranty@MOBI.com.pk

      c-After that you will be contacted from our sales department and they will provide you with all necessary information and will give you closest address where to return your order.

      d-From the moment when clients order gets delivered back to MOBI.com.pk in undamaged condition, money will be refunded to the customer within 30 days or customer will be sent a voucher in the same amount, that can be used for purchasing any products on our web site. Client is the one who chooses whether he prefers a voucher or money refund.

  • Package was delivered to me damaged, how can I refund my loss?

    • If you asked for your package to be insured when ordering, than it is necessary when picking up the order to notify courier service delivery person that the package is damaged and he/she will make a remark of this in their system. After that you need to send us an E-mail with detail description of package, its damage and if possible to include the pictures as well.

      After that we will file a claim at the insurance company agent and when they process our claim and refund us for damage, we will sent you money on your account or you will be able to purchase something else in that value from MOBI.com.pk web site.
